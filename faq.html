Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about finding your next job with RapidCrew.

How do I apply for jobs?

Simply head to our Apply Now page, complete the form, and upload your resume. Our team will review your details and match you with suitable opportunities near you.

Is there a fee to apply?

No. We believe in helping workers succeed. Applying through RapidCrew is completely free for all job seekers.

What kinds of jobs do you offer?

We match applicants with a variety of roles across industries, including Construction, Logistics, Administration, and Marketing, depending on your experience and current openings.

How soon will I hear back?

Most applicants hear from our recruitment team within 3–5 business days. If your profile matches a current opening, we will reach out to schedule your first interview.

Do you require ID or background checks?

Yes. To protect our employers and ensure legal compliance, we require a valid ID and may conduct a standard background check during the placement process.

Can I apply if I live outside of Texas?

Yes! While we are based in Duncanville, TX, we offer nationwide coverage and help match candidates with opportunities all across the United States.
